About Us

A brief history about us.

Welcome to JDNH

Established in 2005, our clinic has only continued to evolve rapidly over the years.Before this establishment ,our Founder used to serve and practice in several places in Bhatpar Rani Town. He is the sole organizer,instigator and architect of this establishment.

At the time of Establishment there were very limited staffs and patients.We had very few divisions and limited equipment and treatment scalability. With the evolvement of building infrastructure few more divisions were added and new machines and tools were installed which resulted in more and more number of people connecting with us and eventually increase in patients' trust over the years.
